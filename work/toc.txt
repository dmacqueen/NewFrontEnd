A "Table of Contents" for the work directory.

This directory contains notes on various random topics related to MsML (MacQueen's own version
of successor ML) and its implementation in the New Front End project.

Files:

bug319.txt
   Discussion of the problem underlying bugs 319/344 that caused me to realize that a deeper
   reworking of the module implementation was required, hence New Front End.

intro.txt
   Some "introductory" thoughts.  Goals, strategy, principles.

harperismus.txt
   Notes on Harper, Crary, et al ideas about type-theoretic formal semantics.
   The question is how these ideas are relevant to a new implementation.

journal.txt
    A journal of work done.  Patchy.
